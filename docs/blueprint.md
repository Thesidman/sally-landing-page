# **App Name**: SALLY: AI Sales Agent

## Core Features:

- Autonomous Lead Engagement: SALLY autonomously keeps sales conversations alive within WhatsApp and other channels, using a reasoning tool to engage leads based on context and past interactions.
- Contextual Conversation Awareness: Reads and remembers conversation details across multiple channels to provide relevant and timely follow-ups, ensuring no lead goes cold.
- AI-Powered Reply Generation: Generates personalized and engaging replies to leads based on conversation history and sales context, improving reply rates and lead engagement.
- Intelligent Lead Prioritization: Prioritizes leads based on their likelihood to convert, ensuring sales teams focus on the most promising opportunities.
- Automated Demo Booking: Proposes and books demo slots for leads directly within the chat, streamlining the sales process and increasing conversion rates.
- Sales Playbook Integration: Integrates with sales playbooks to automatically share relevant case studies, pricing decks, and other resources with leads.
- Performance Analytics: Tracks and analyzes reply rates, lead engagement, and other key metrics to provide insights into sales performance and optimize strategies.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary brand color: WhatsApp green (#25D366) for main CTAs, accents, and icons.
- Mint glow/background tints: #E9FFF4 and #D7FBE9 for soft gradients and glows.
- Secondary AI accent: Soft lavender (#E6DEFF) used subtly in gradients, tags, and highlights.
- Warm accent: Soft coral (#FFC3B0) sparingly in chips/metrics.
- Base background: White (#FFFFFF) with faint mint-to-white gradient.
- Text colors: Almost-black (#0B0F0D) for headings, medium grey (#5F6570) for body copy.
- Font pairing: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for body text and 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for titles
- Airy layout with generous padding, centered hero section.
- Use small, clear icons in WhatsApp green for key actions and elements.
- Subtle animations for button hovers and card transitions.
